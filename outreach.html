Content Brief Template

Send This to Confirmed Creators

Campaign Overview

This Ramadan, OKX — in partnership with Dubai Cares and Volunteer Emirates — is donating AED 1,000,000 to distribute 10,000 school kits to children in need across the region.

This is a 100% CSR/charitable campaign. You are participating as a community voice — not as a crypto promoter. Your content should feel personal, authentic, and cause-driven.

Your Deliverables

🎬
1 × Instagram Reel (60–90 seconds)Your authentic take on why this Ramadan cause matters to you. No script required — speak from the heart. Must include: mention of Dubai Cares + campaign link in caption.
📲
2 × Instagram StoriesAt least one Story must include the campaign link (link sticker or swipe-up). You can repost the Reel as a Story + add a link, or create fresh Stories about the cause.

Campaign Link (add to every Story)

https://okx.com/en-ae/campaigns/dubai-cares-ramadan

Hashtags (Maximum 2)

#OKXDubaiCares    #RamadanGiving

Content Direction — DO SAY ✅

  • "Charity campaign" / "CSR initiative"
  • "Ramadan giving" / "giving back this Ramadan"
  • "Dubai Cares partnership" / "school kits for children"
  • "OKX community giving back"
  • Your personal story of giving or what this cause means to you

Content Direction — DO NOT SAY ❌

  • "Crypto promotion" / "trading platform" / "OKX exchange"
  • "Invest" / "buy" / "sell" / "token" / "blockchain"
  • Any financial advice or investment direction
  • Price predictions or market commentary

Disclosure Requirement (UAE VARA)

If you are receiving compensation: your post must include #Ad or #Sponsored as required by UAE regulations. Pro-bono participants do not need to disclose. When in doubt, ask TiKiT before posting.

Approval Process

  • All content must be shared with TiKiT for review before posting
  • TiKiT reviews within 4 hours (business hours) / 12 hours (overnight)
  • OKX may request minor edits — this is standard, not personal
  • Once approved: post and tag @okx_official

Posting Window

  • Optimal: 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM UAE time (post-iftar engagement peak)
  • Avoid: 2 AM – 6 AM UAE (low engagement window)
  • Coordinate with TiKiT to avoid content clashing with other creators

Contact

Any questions → contact your TiKiT point of contact directly on WhatsApp or email. We're responsive 7 days a week during Ramadan. 🌙
