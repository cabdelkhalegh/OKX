This Ramadan, OKX — in partnership with Dubai Cares and Volunteer Emirates — is donating AED 1,000,000 to distribute 10,000 school kits to children in need across the region.

This is a 100% CSR/charitable campaign. You are participating as a community voice — not as a crypto promoter. Your content should feel personal, authentic, and cause-driven.

Your Deliverables

🎬
1 × Instagram Reel (60–90 seconds)Your authentic take on why this Ramadan cause matters to you. No script required — speak from the heart. Must include: mention of Dubai Cares + campaign link in caption.
📲
2 × Instagram StoriesAt least one Story must include the campaign link (link sticker or swipe-up). You can repost the Reel as a Story + add a link, or create fresh Stories about the cause.

If you are receiving compensation: your post must include #Ad or #Sponsored as required by UAE regulations. Pro-bono participants do not need to disclose. When in doubt, ask TiKiT before posting.

  • OKX may request minor edits — this is standard, not personal
  • Once approved: post and tag @okx_official

Posting Window

  • Optimal: 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM UAE time (post-iftar engagement peak)
